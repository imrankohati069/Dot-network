Whitepaper

Introduction

Papa Network is a decentralized, community-driven platform that allows everyday users to earn tokens directly from their mobile phones without the need for expensive mining hardware.

Mission Statement

Empowering everyday users to participate in the future of digital currency through simple actions and community engagement.

How It Works

  • Users install the app
  • Tap the "Start Earning" button
  • A 24-hour timer runs, rewarding 1 Papa per day
  • Users earn 1 extra Dots per referral
  • Daily check-ins maintain mining status

Technology

Papa Network is mobile-first and utilizes Firebase for backend services. The system is Web3-ready for future smart contract integration on Binance Smart Chain or similar networks.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1 billion
  • Community Airdrop: 60% (Daily unlock)
  • Marketing: 10% (Partnership & promotions)
  • Team & Developers: 10% (6 months lock)
  • Liquidity Pool: 10% (DEX listings)
  • Reserve: 10% (Governance & upgrades)

Referral Model

Users earn bonus Dots for each referral who is actively mining. Anti-bot and fraud detection systems will ensure only genuine activity is rewarded.

Roadmap

  • Q3 2025: App Launch with Mining System
  • Q4 2025: User Growth via Referrals
  • Q1 2026: Token Deployment (BSC/ERC-20)
  • Q2 2026: Airdrop Claim Start + Wallet Integration
  • Q3 2026: Listing on DEX (like PancakeSwap)

Vision

Papa Network aims to bring the blockchain revolution to every mobile user in a simple, secure, and investment-free manner.
